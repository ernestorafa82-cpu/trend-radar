Independent platforms

Each platform has its own 24/7 worker agent.

These are not dashboards with AI decoration. They are two independent Shopify operations platforms designed to communicate when the agency needs a full operational loop: live-store vigilance, controlled change, evidence, approval and post-action confidence.

Release assurance

Checkout Shield

Agent: Operator Zero

Checkout Shield is the control platform for critical Shopify changes. Operator Zero works 24/7 across releases, checkout risk, evidence, blockers, revenue truth and sign-off workflows.

  • Controls checkout, payments, shipping, discounts, tracking, pixels, thank you / order status, post-purchase and revenue truth.
  • Turns technical signals into operational decisions: proceed, block, monitor or sign off with conditions.
  • Prepares evidence packs, release assessments, audit trails, client handoff and owner-review context.
  • Escalates sensitive actions for human approval instead of pushing dangerous auto-approval.
Revenue protection

Merchant Sentinel

Agent: Sentinel worker

Merchant Sentinel is the live-store vigilance platform for Shopify agencies. Its worker agent monitors operational signals, detects incidents, prioritizes risk and helps the agency act before revenue damage becomes visible to the client.

  • Watches multi-store health, checkout signals, payment degradation, revenue risk, operational drift and critical alerts.
  • Transforms Shopify signals into incidents, evidence, impact context and next actions.
  • Supports recurring agency operations, retainer value, client reporting and faster incident response.
  • Communicates with Checkout Shield when a detected issue needs a controlled release or sensitive action.
